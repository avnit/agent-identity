# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility
Full-time employees in roles classified as remote-eligible may work from any
approved location within their assigned country of employment. Changes of
country require HR approval and may have tax and compliance implications.

## Workspace requirements
- A private, quiet workspace suitable for handling confidential information.
- Reliable internet (minimum 25 Mbps down / 5 Mbps up).
- Company-issued laptop, used only by the employee.
- VPN connection when accessing internal systems.

## Hours and availability
Core collaboration hours are 10:00–15:00 in the employee's primary timezone.
Employees are expected to be reachable during these hours unless approved PTO
is scheduled.

## Equipment stipend
A one-time $500 stipend is available for home office setup (desk, chair,
monitor). Submit receipts via the expense system within 90 days of role
start.

## Security
- Lock your screen when stepping away.
- Never work on customer data over public Wi-Fi without the VPN.
- Report lost or stolen devices to IT Security within 24 hours.
