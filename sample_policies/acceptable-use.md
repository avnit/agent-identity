# Acceptable Use Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines acceptable use of ASB Solutions Group information systems,
including email, cloud services, and internal applications.

## Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties with access to
company systems.

## Rules
1. Company systems are provided primarily for business use. Limited personal
   use is permitted if it does not interfere with work duties.
2. Do not share passwords, API keys, or authentication tokens.
3. Do not install unapproved software on company-managed devices.
4. Do not use company systems to access, store, or transmit unlawful,
   harassing, or sexually explicit material.
5. All traffic may be monitored for security and compliance purposes.

## Enforcement
Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination,
and referral to law enforcement where applicable.
